Predicting PV Rooftop Solar Installations
Dive into the Details
Objective
Using machine learning to predict the future number of solar rooftop installs in the USA

Initial Hypothesis
The volume of rooftop PV solar installations within each county is affected by several key market factors, including:
  • Housing type populations in each county (numbers of single families, townhouses, commercial)
  • Party registration
  • Local electricity rates
  • Education levels
  • Availability and reviews of local installation contractors
  • Installation price
